Our History

Founded in 2010, TechCon has grown from a local meetup of 200 tech enthusiasts to an international conference attracting over 10,000 attendees annually. What began as a small gathering in Silicon Valley has become the premier event for technology innovation worldwide.

Last year's conference featured 150+ sessions across 12 tracks, with keynote addresses from industry pioneers like Elon Musk and Tim Berners-Lee.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between emerging technologies and practical implementation. We bring together:

  • Visionaries shaping the future of tech
  • Developers building tomorrow's solutions
  • Business leaders driving digital transformation

Our goal is to foster collaboration that accelerates meaningful technological progress.
